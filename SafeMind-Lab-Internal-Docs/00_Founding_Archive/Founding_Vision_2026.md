# Founding Vision – 2026
Status: Committed

## Mission
SafeMind Lab exists to provide the secure control plane for autonomous systems,
ensuring that as intelligence becomes autonomous, it remains accountable, safe,
and legally deployable.

## Core Thesis
Autonomy will not fail due to lack of intelligence.
It will fail due to lack of control, auditability, and liability containment.

## The Trust Gap
As agents and physical AI gain autonomy, enterprises lose visibility and control.
SafeMind closes this gap by enforcing identity, logic, and resource boundaries.
